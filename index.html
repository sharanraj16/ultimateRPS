Game Rules

In Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock, each player chooses one of five options: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock. The game is played with the following rules:

  • Rock crushes Scissors and crushes Lizard.
  • Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock.
  • Scissors cuts Paper and decapitates Lizard.
  • Lizard eats Paper and poisons Spock.
  • Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock.

The player with the winning choice scores a point. First to reach 10 points wins the game!
